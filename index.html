Beyond the dashboard.

Your dashboard reports values. Observant Drive watches how those values behave together.

Using live OBD-II data, GPS, and learned vehicle baselines, Observant Drive helps transform vehicle data into meaningful insight by highlighting changes in battery health, charging behavior, engine warm-up, coolant temperature, fuel trims, airflow, boost, engine load, and long-term vehicle trends.

Rather than treating every sensor as an isolated number, Observant Drive compares related systems over time to help highlight patterns, drift, and early signs that deserve attention.

Whether you're commuting to work, taking a road trip, maintaining a daily driver or performance vehicle, or simply curious about how your car behaves, Observant Drive is designed to help you better understand your vehicle before small changes become bigger problems.
