Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Observant Drive is an Android application developed by Observant Systems LLC to help drivers better understand vehicle health using compatible OBD-II adapters and supported Android device sensors.

Information the App Uses

Depending on the features you choose to use, Observant Drive may access:

  • Bluetooth (to communicate with compatible OBD-II adapters)
  • Location (required by Android for Bluetooth scanning and optional GPS-based features)
  • Vehicle telemetry provided by your OBD-II adapter
  • Supported Android device sensors used by app features

Your Data

Observant Drive is designed to process vehicle telemetry primarily on your device. Vehicle information, learned baselines, observations, historical trends, and health reports remain on your device unless you intentionally choose to export data or submit feedback.

Observant Systems does not sell your personal information.

Feedback & Exports

If you choose to use the in-app Send Feedback feature, contact us by email, or export information from the app, only the information you intentionally choose to send is shared with Observant Systems to help improve the software.

Closed Testing

Observant Drive is currently provided as a testing release. Features, functionality, and data handling may evolve as the software continues to develop.
